About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Pet Services & Care began as a small pet grooming salon with a big heart. What started as a passion project has grown into a full-service pet care center dedicated to providing the highest quality services and products for your furry family members.

Our Mission

We believe pets deserve the same level of care and attention as any other family member. Our mission is to enhance the lives of pets and their owners through exceptional services, premium products, and expert advice in a welcoming, loving environment.

Our Team

Our certified pet care specialists bring years of experience and genuine love for animals to every interaction. From groomers to trainers, each team member undergoes rigorous training to ensure your pet receives the best possible care.
